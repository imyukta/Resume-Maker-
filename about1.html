   Objective

The main objective of this website "Resume Maker" is to manage the details of manage the details of the resume ,qualifications and skills. It manages the information resume and indivisual.This website is built totally at administrative end and thus only the administrator guaranteed the access.The purpose of the website is to reduce manual work for managing the information of the resume .It keeps the records of the skills and qualifications of the user.

   Features

  • Different types of templates of resume are provided.
  • User can create an account using email address and can be modified any time .
  • Use of spell checker so that no mistake can occur.
  • The resume can be downloaded in the form of pdf.
  • No need of any formal knowledge for writing resume.
  • Data can be accessed by the user himself and the administrator
